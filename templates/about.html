About Dave Foucar

Dave has had a successful career in corporate finance but his heart has always been in ministry. He has spent decades in church leadership teaching Sunday School, Bible Studies, and Small Groups for all ages. He has been a lead speaker and seminar leader at Couple's Retreats, Men's Retreats, and Family and Youth Camps.

Dave has been married for 35 years (as of 2021). He and his wife, Debbie, have three adult daughters but ONE favorite son-in-law, Cameron. Each of them bring endless joy as they expand their families and careers.
